Education

Make computer science a core subject in public schools

Started by Priya Nair · Addressed to: Department of Education · Started May 2026

In 2026, nearly every professional field intersects with computing. Yet most public school students graduate without a single mandatory computer science class. The subject is treated as an elective, available in well-funded districts and absent in underfunded ones — a digital divide that tracks closely with race and income.

This is not a technology problem. It is an equity problem.

What we're asking for

This petition calls for computer science to be added to the national core curriculum for Grades 6 through 12 — alongside mathematics, science, and language arts. Not as an elective. As a required subject with trained teachers, dedicated instructional time, and consistent standards across all public schools.

With AI tools now embedded in nearly every industry, computational literacy is not optional — it is foundational. Many countries have already made this shift. The question is whether we are willing to make it universal.
