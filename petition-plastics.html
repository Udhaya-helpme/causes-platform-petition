Environment

Phase out single-use plastics from food service by 2027

Started by Carlos Mendes · Addressed to: Environmental Protection Agency · Started April 2026

Food service is the single largest contributor to single-use plastic waste — accounting for an estimated 40% of all plastic packaging that ends up in landfills and waterways each year. Cups, lids, straws, cutlery, clamshell containers: most are used once, for minutes, and persist in the environment for centuries.

Viable, cost-competitive alternatives now exist across every category. The barrier is not technology — it is regulatory will.

What we're asking for

This petition calls for a binding federal phase-out of single-use plastics in food service by January 1, 2027, paired with a transition support fund for small businesses. Cities and states that have enacted bans have seen measurable reductions in coastal and inland plastic pollution within 18 months of implementation.

National-level action is the only way to close the gaps left by a patchwork of local rules. Sign to demand federal leadership on this issue.
